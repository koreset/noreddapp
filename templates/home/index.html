No REDD in Africa Network

An African civil society and community-based organizations and individuals opposed to all forms of REDD and REDD-Type projects. NRAN sees REDD as a false climate solution that merely permits polluters to pollute and further harm the climate. We work to defend forest-dependent communities while promoting community-based forest protection. NRAN works with similar networks and groups in other regions of the world.
